Detailed Description

Represents a cost of an item.

Parameters

taxa Compulsory contribution to state revenue.
grossDifference between revenue and the cost of product.
disDiscount. A deduction from the usual cost of product.
netamtOverall cost of product for a customer.
qtyQuantity (amount) of particullar item customer wants to buy.
